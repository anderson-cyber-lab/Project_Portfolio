# Sprint 1: Cybersecurity Career Self-Assessment & Introduction

## Overview

Completed a structured career self-assessment and professional introduction as the foundational sprint of the TripleTen Cybersecurity program. Delivered as a Google Slides presentation, this project established a personal baseline — mapping existing skills, identifying knowledge gaps, and setting goals for the program ahead.

## Objectives

- Reflect on prior experience and transferable skills relevant to cybersecurity
- Identify target roles and career pathways within the cybersecurity field
- Define personal learning goals and success metrics for the program
- Present a professional self-introduction for peer and instructor review

## Key Topics Covered

- **Background & Transferable Skills** — Prior professional experience and how it applies to cybersecurity roles
- **Target Career Path** — Identified areas of interest including SOC Analysis, Penetration Testing, and Incident Response
- **Self-Assessment** — Honest evaluation of current technical strengths and areas for development
- **Program Goals** — Defined objectives for completing the TripleTen curriculum and achieving CompTIA Security+ certification
- **Cybersecurity Landscape** — Overview of the field, in-demand roles, and the threat environment motivating entry into the profession

## Deliverable

Presented as a Google Slides deck covering personal background, motivations for entering cybersecurity, self-assessed skill gaps, and a structured plan for growth throughout the program.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Professional self-presentation and communication
- Career planning and goal setting in a technical field
- Self-directed learning mindset
- Understanding of cybersecurity career pathways and role landscape
